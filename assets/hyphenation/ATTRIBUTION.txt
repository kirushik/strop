Hyphenation pattern data — attribution and licenses
====================================================

The two dictionary files in this directory are compiled (bincode) forms of
TeX hyphenation patterns from the hyph-utf8 package
(https://www.hyphenation.org/tex, https://github.com/hyphenation/tex-hyphen),
as bundled by the `hyphenation` Rust crate v0.8.4
(https://github.com/tapeinosyne/hyphenation, crate code Apache-2.0/MIT).

They are distributed with Strop as SEPARATE, INDEPENDENT DATA FILES, loaded
at runtime; each keeps its own license below (GPLv3 §5 "mere aggregation").
They are never compiled into the Strop binary.

ru.standard.bincode
