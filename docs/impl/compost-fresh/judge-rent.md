# Judge — the rent lens

Standard: every element pays rent against a typed divider at the bottom of the
document, or dies. Chrome over convention kills. Designs judged as critiqued;
an unanswered kill-shot counts against.

## Ranking

1. tail · 2. spartan · 3. fold · 4. margin · 5. drawer · 6. verso

## The audit

**tail — winner.** The only design whose thesis *is* this lens: "the null
baseline, dignified." Empty state pixel-identical to shipping nothing; four
elements, each retiring a named baseline failure (seam → 1/2/6, verb → 3,
composer → 4, provenance → 5). The adoption gesture — select your existing
pile, one chord, the tool has learned your scrap line — converts convention to
structure at zero new habit. Both panels returned "needs-surgery, beats," and
the surgery is subtraction plus spec-completion: restore the two-chip contract
(a revert to shipped behavior), add the excursion latch (a solved house
problem), specify undo, demote the standing provenance chips (removing chrome
is rent-positive surgery). The in-fiction Spartan, default vote shelve,
conceded: "the only direction where I lose it cleanly." The kill-shots (silent
park receipt, Esc mauling a live caret, one-chip regression, F4 aimed at the
rare case, jot/annotate classification) are real, but none touches the
structural core, and independent convergence with the house's prior Option B
corroborates the direction.

**spartan — 2nd.** The purest discipline in the field, written to disarm this
lens; the scope law is one sentence and the empty state is genuinely nothing.
But the soul is a magic string and the phrase-collision kill-shot stands
unanswered: a legal line of dialogue silently hijacks the boundary,
reintroducing baseline failure 1 — the design can regress *below* the baseline
it exists to beat. The jot transits prose and a lull checkpoint can immortalize
it as manuscript. Its own graft list ("the element wears the sentence")
converts it into tail; as critiqued, it is tail with a defective boundary and a
worse jot.

**fold — 3rd.** The best resting silhouette (zero chrome until used) and the
best jot in the batch. But parking-in-place is a geometry no writer in the
practice inventory performs; at documented cut volume the draft striates and
the only clean view is read-only; fold-as-atom installs four exceptions to
"typing types" at the exact center of the surface. The bet's unique yield
serves the ~1–2% flow; its unique cost taxes the daily one. A bad trade,
honestly confessed.

**margin — 4th.** The cheapest conceivable scope win — reuse the one boundary
every subsystem already respects — and that insight should survive somewhere.
But it puts unbounded storage in a rationed lane whose defining property is
eviction; "beside the seam it left" fails under exactly the load the feature
invites; the sweep ritual has no home while the dead pile reads contiguously;
seeds mis-anchor. The interface panel's own clause fires: against a tail
rival the verdict hardens to fatal, and a tail rival exists.

**drawer — 5th.** Failed the rent test inside its own critique: delete the
drawer's rendering, keep the third region in-flow at the tail, and every
scorecard win survives except 1600 pt side-by-side. That single moment is the
entire exclusive rent of a permanent edge, a self-confessed "most modal object
in the product," a width-forked click law, and a Spike-grade chord-only jot.
Best organs (region model, verb gradient, scope-labelled count), wrong body.

**verso — 6th.** Two constitutional violations as specced (writer's prose worn
as chrome on the dog-ear; record grammar on living text) and the one failure
mode worse than shipping nothing: silent wrong-face drafting exiles manuscript
prose from export, counts, AI and cold read, discovered at deadline or never.
The baseline fails loudly by surplus; verso fails silently by absence. The
interface panel's "fatal" holds under this lens; the excised-flip remainder is
the tail design, already on the table.

## Shelve: weak

Baseline failures 1, 2 and 6 are structural: no convention can make the tool
know the boundary, keep the reviewer out of the pile, scope the count, or
version the geometry. Only a machine-known boundary fixes them, and tail
delivers one whose resting cost is zero — a scrapless document is the null
baseline to the pixel. When both adversarial shelve-advocates concede on the
same design, shelve is not serious.

## Grafts onto tail

1. **Arrival, not decrement** (spartan): parked text visibly departs toward
   the foot via the shipped re-pack slide; the chip pulses as an event.
2. **Atomic history inverse** (spartan/verso): park/jot is one history
   operation; immediate ctrl-Z restores verbatim and evaporates a just-born
   seam; jot transit never lands in a checkpoint.
3. **Scope-labelled count control** (drawer): `draft · 1,842` / `scraps · 312`
   by caret region — P12, and mid-pile screenshots self-identify.
4. **Enter grows the jot** (drawer): the composer commits on Esc/click-away
   only; Enter extends the line — no chopped thoughts.
5. **Diagnosis-card retirement on park** (margin): a card anchored in a parked
   passage retires with it; Put back re-eligibilizes.
